What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software, and data, from attack, damage, or unauthorized access. This includes protecting against cyber threats such as hacking, malware, and phishing attacks.

Why is Cybersecurity Important?

Cybersecurity is important because it helps to protect sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access to systems. This can help to prevent identity theft, financial fraud, and other types of cybercrime. Additionally, it can help to protect against attacks that could cripple entire systems or networks.

How Can You Protect Yourself Online?

There are several ways to protect yourself online, including: using strong passwords, keeping software and security systems up to date, being cautious when clicking on links or downloading attachments, and being wary of phishing scams. Additionally, you can use a virtual private network (VPN) to encrypt your internet connection and protect your data from being intercepted.
